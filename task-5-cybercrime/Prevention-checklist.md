## Prevention Checklist: “Stay Safe Online”

**Actionable Safety Tips**

1.	Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA). 
2.	Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts or making transactions. 
3.	Always verify links and emails before clicking to avoid phishing scams. 
4.	Keep your devices updated with the latest software and security patches. 
5.	Never share sensitive information like OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone. 
6.	Download apps only from trusted sources and check permissions before installing. 
7.	Log out from shared devices and avoid saving passwords on public systems. 
8.	Regularly check your bank account and transaction history for any suspicious activity. 
    Financial / UPI Safety Tips
9.	Do not approve unknown UPI collect requests—fraudsters may trick you into paying instead of receiving money. 
10.	Never scan random QR codes to receive money—QR codes are used only for sending payments.
