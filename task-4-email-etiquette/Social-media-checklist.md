## SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST

## Do's

•	Do adjust your privacy settings to control who can view your posts and personal information.

•	Do think before posting—ensure your content reflects a positive and professional image.

•	Do connect with professors, industry professionals, and alumni to build a meaningful network.

•	Do take regular breaks and set time limits to maintain a healthy balance between online and offline life.

•	Do report and block harmful accounts or instances of cyberbullying to protect yourself and others.


## Don'ts

•	Don't share private details such as your home address, phone number, or financial information publicly.

•	Don't engage in online arguments or post offensive comments that could harm your reputation.

•	Don't share unverified news or misinformation that could mislead others.

•	Don't post when angry or upset—wait until you are calm to avoid impulsive posts.

•	Don't neglect your digital footprint—avoid content that could negatively impact future job or internship opportunities.
