## Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio, created as part of the CSE0001 - Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have completed five tasks that demonstrate my understanding of essential digital literacy concepts, including creating professional online profiles, communicating safely online, and helping peers navigate the digital world.

This project showcases my ability to:
- Create engaging visual content for awareness campaigns
- Build and maintain professional digital profiles
- Utilize coding and collaboration platforms effectively
- Practice professional email communication
- Understand and prevent cybercrime threats
---
## Module Summaries

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a visual infographic using Canva to educate peers about digital literacy. The infographic covers three key topics: what digital literacy is, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. This visual resource helps batchmates understand the importance of being digitally literate in today's academic and professional environment.

### Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
Established professional profiles on three platforms: GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Set up GitHub profile README with personal details and academic interests. Updated LinkedIn Education section with current degree and institution details. These platforms will serve as the foundation for my digital presence throughout my engineering journey.

### Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner-level coding challenge on HackerRank to practice programming skills. Created a Google Form with 5 questions as a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz for batchmates. These tools will help me collaborate with peers and develop technical skills essential for academic success.

### Task 4: Email Etiquette & Social Media Checklist
Drafted two professional emails: one requesting assignment extension from a professor, and another expressing internship interest to a company. Created a comprehensive Do's and Don'ts checklist for social media use as a college student. This task reinforced the importance of maintaining professionalism in digital communication.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study
Researched phishing as a cybercrime type and wrote a detailed case study covering how it happens, who is targeted, and its consequences. Created a "Stay Safe Online" prevention checklist with actionable tips for college students in India, including UPI safety measures and reporting channels like the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and Helpline 1930.
